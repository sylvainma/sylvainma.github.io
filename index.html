Who am I?

My name is Sylvain Marchienne and I graduated in Computer Science and Machine Learning at Georgia Tech. As a Machine Learning Engineer, I focus on modeling and productionizing valuable models.

Experience

In 2017, I spent seven months at craft ai working on a predictive solution for urban waste management for the city of Paris. I also co-founded Dataventure, a Data Science student organization. Then I flew to China for a semester where I studied leadership at Shanghai University and created Explorify, a Machine Learning based photography web application.

In 2018, I finished my first master's degree at the University of Technology of Compiègne. Then in 2019, I worked half a year on Video Summarization at GoPro where I trained Deep Learning models to detect highlights in users' videos. Later on I published Summarizer, a research package implementing state-of-the-art models in Video Summarization. Finally, in May 2020 I graduated in Computer Science and Machine Learning from Georgia Institute of Technology.
